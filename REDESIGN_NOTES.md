# Redesign notes

## What changed

- Replaced the one-page promotional composition with real route-separated pages.
- Removed the centered text-over-image hero and used an asymmetric editorial split.
- Replaced the repeated three-card grid with a featured monthly lot and a compact product ledger.
- Reduced rounded containers, shadows, decorative badges, and repeated CTA blocks.
- Built a warm paper, coffee, oxide, and muted gold palette without gradients or pure black.
- Used a restrained grotesk and monospaced labeling system to evoke a showroom catalogue.
- Kept motion limited to page entry, navigation reveal, and a slow origin ticker.
- Added Romanian and Russian route parity, including product deep links.

## MVP states

- Collection: loading skeleton, search, reset, and empty state.
- Contact form: field errors, sending, success, and form reset.
- Navigation: active state, localized route switching, and mobile overlay.
- Responsive: desktop, tablet, and 390 px mobile layouts without horizontal overflow.

## Reference boundary

The site borrows the reference's editorial rhythm, premium restraint, and dark/light contrast. It does not copy CoffeeShare branding, text, logos, or page structure verbatim.
